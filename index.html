Fabulous stuff... and nonsense

Marshall and Marshall is a beautiful shop located in Brighouse
and Skipton, stocking a range of original, fun and high quality gifts,
home accessories, jewellery and clothing. Looking at the shop window
you’d be forgiven for thinking it looks expensive, but everything’s very
affordable too.

We like to make people smile...

We love our gorgeous shops, and so do our customers.
We enjoy a loyal customer following because they know they’ll find
what they’re looking for every time they visit us.

Our whole range has been carefully selected to give you a choice
of original, uplifting and funny gifts with individuality, and our expert
team are here to help you choose something that’s just right.

... and make life easy

To save you time, most items come attractively presented in a
gift bag with tissue and organza, so you won’t even need to buy

pretty soon!
