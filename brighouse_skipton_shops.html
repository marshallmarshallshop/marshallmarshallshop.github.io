The Shop at Brighouse


Our original shop opened in June 2006, and we’ve been giving our customers a friendly welcome ever since. Decorated as beautifully as if it was our own home, this shop is a little breath of fresh air. You’ll never feel pushed to buy anything, but the fact is, you’ll be so bowled over with choice that you won’t know what to choose – that’s where our team will help you with honest opinions to make that all important decision.

Where to begin?

When you visit our Brighouse shop, your eyes will open wide as you try to take in all the beautiful things on display.


Depending on what you’re looking for, you’ll be drawn to the eye-popping display of jewellery in the centre of the room, but make sure you don’t miss anything on the shelves and dressers. You may need to walk around a few times to see everything!


Brigitte, Denise, Lesley and Kimberley will be there to help you with anything you need, and you’ll find our fitting rooms through the curtain at the back of the shop if you’d like to try anything on.

The shop at Skipton


Opened in July 2012, the stunning Skipton shop has been fitted to a very high standard. We want our customers to feel that Marshall and Marshall is a home from home where they’ll be surrounded by beautiful things and will always receive a friendly welcome.

Where to begin?

When you visit our Skipton shop, enter through our double doors and feast your eyes on our beautiful displays with floor length mirrors, luxurious fitting rooms (with a comfy chair of course), and walls stacked high with fabulous stuff and nonsense, all beautifully lit by sparkling chandeliers.


Brigitte, Michelle, Lesley and Kimberley will be there to help you with anything you need, and feel free to try anything on in our luxurious fitting rooms.

How do I get there?

Find us in the heart of popular Skipton at 18a, Swadford Street, Skipton BD23 1RD

Parking

Skipton is small town and all parking is generally close by. The closest parking to the shop is the Waller Hill car park in the bus station where you can park for up to two hours, or the Pop and Shop scheme allows you to park behind Skipton Town Hall for just 20p for half an hour.

Opening times

We’re open Monday to Saturday from 10am to 5.30pm and Sundays from 11am to 4pm.
